# Proposals — Longrun

- New proposals: `.proposals/YYYYMMDD-title.md`. Archived (implemented or
  rejected): `.proposals/archived/`.
- Frontmatter `status:` one of `draft`, `approved`, `rejected`, `implemented`.
- Proposal-worthy here: anything on spec.md's "Out (later)" list (terminal
  input, search, log files, CLI companion, sync, start-order dependencies),
  new dependencies, storage-format changes, richer restart policies.
- Small in-scope discoveries don't need a proposal — a line in the task
  file's Decisions section is enough.
